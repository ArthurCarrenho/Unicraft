Info - English

UniCraft is a project designed to show that not even the best university in Brazil in the world is 100% safe.

Created initially as UspCraft, the UniCraft project started with the addition of UnB, UniFacef, and UniCamp.

To play just click on the desired college logo. Compatible natively with Firefox

To play in chrome, use the --disable-xss-auditor flag when you open it
